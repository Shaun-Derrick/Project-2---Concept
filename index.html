Mission

Our goal is to make recycling accessible to everyone while making the process as easy and quick as possible.
We believe that by increasing the accessibility of recycling facilities it will enable more people to recycle.
If we enable more people to recycle there will be fewer recycleables reaching the landfill and therefore it will provide a cleaner and healthier environment.
